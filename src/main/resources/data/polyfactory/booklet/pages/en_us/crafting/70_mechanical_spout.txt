<yellow><item 'polyfactory:mechanical_spout'></> is a 1x2x1 machine block that allows you to craft things with liquids.
<nl2>
This block sources it's fluid from the top, additionally acting as a weaker <item 'polyfactory:mechanical_pump'> with
effect distance of 8. You can use <item 'polyfactory:fluid_tank'>s, Portable Fluid Tanks or <item 'polyfactory:pipe'>s
to provide it.
<nl2>
This block requires rotational energy to work, with its stress requirement being equal to 1 unit when active.
<nl>
Conveyors can also directly pull from / push to this block, as long they are oriented correctly.
