<yellow>Portable Fluid Tank</> is a portable variant of the <item 'polyfactory:fluid_tank'> block.
<nl2>
It can store up to 4 buckets/blocks worth of fluid. It also can store different types of fluid at the same time,
through some of them might react with each other. Portable Fluid Tank can only be accessed from only a single side,
as indicated by its opening.
<nl>
When broken, fluids will be contained within the item. They can then be placed again or used by items such as
<item 'polyfactory:pressure_fluid_gun'>.
