<yellow><item 'polyfactory:item_packer'></> is as storage interface block, that exposes items stored within
other items, such as Shulker Boxes and Bundles.
<nl2>
It can be interfaced with blocks such as <item 'polyfactory:hopper'> and <item 'polyfactory:funnel'>.
<nl>
To interact with item-attached storage, you need to interface with solid side of the <item 'polyfactory:item_packer'>.
You will be able to take and put items into it.
<nl>
To change the held item within the <item 'polyfactory:item_packer'>, you need to interact with "open" sides
(aka ones where you see the items with). Easiest way to do so would be by using Funnels.
