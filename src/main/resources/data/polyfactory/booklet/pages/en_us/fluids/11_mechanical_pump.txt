<yellow><item 'polyfactory:pump'></> is a block that powers the <u><blue><pagelink './10_copper_pipe'>pipe fluid transport</*>.
<nl>
It's directional block, making its facing direction control where fluids get pumped / pulled form.
The fluid flow direction is indicated with a blue arrow.
<nl2>
The range of the pump that it can push fluids through pipe is always equal to 32 blocks,
however depending on provided to it speed the strength might vary.
Additionally, it always pulls 5 units of stress on the connected system,
independent on the speed of it.
<nl>
To connect to it, you need to use a gear on the side.
<nl2>
<item 'polyfactory:pump'> act similarly to pipes, being able to pull, push and store fluids within them.
